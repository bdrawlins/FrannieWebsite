Her Work

Built Around People

Frannie's work has always stretched beyond ordinary party entertainment. She shows up for children, parents, service members, nurses, firefighters, police officers, veterans, neighbors, and families who need a bright moment that feels personal.

Her events can be silly, sweet, patriotic, seasonal, or wildly colorful, but the center is always the same: helping people feel celebrated.

Favorite Places to Serve

  • Birthday parties, holidays, and family events
  • Hospitals and care settings
  • Military and veteran events
  • Firefighter, police, and hometown hero gatherings
